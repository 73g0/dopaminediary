dopamine diary : 

i am an addict

13th of August, 2022


I am addicted to media consumption. I've tried many different approaches to minimize my consumption but I have always found myself in the same spot after some time, which is that I don't have control.
I know that there always is the one moment in which you decide to "press play", sometimes literally and sometimes in the form of starting an application but I am not able to isolate this moment and really think about wether I want to do what I am about to do and how it will affect my life.

Don't get me wrong. Media consumption can be very relaxing and good in order to wind down but the reason why I want to minimize the time I spend on media consumption is that I could use this time for things that are even better for e.g. relaxation.

I will start with only podcast consumption for today and will try to ramp up slowly!
