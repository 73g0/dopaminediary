espresso shot porridge & banana-almond-proteinshake

7th of August, 2022


"Perfect" is a word that I do not like to use.
I don't like to use it, because its definition does not correspond to the general use of it. People use "perfect" in order to describe something that is very good but might be far from perfect.

Nevertheless, I created the perfect breakfast:
Prepare your porridge the way you like it best. Add an espresso shot, cinnamon and honey and add some fruit. I recommend an apple and dried dates. That's my espresso shot porridge!
For the shake you just mix together a banana, preferably ripe, a few dried dates, almond butter, protein-powder, preferably vanilla flavour, and milk!
