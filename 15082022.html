dopamine diary : 

no shortcuts in life

15th of August, 2022


If you see someone win (doesn't matter in which way), you are tempted to think that there was a shortcut involved.
Your brain always searches for the path of least resistance even if it is not the truth and in the winning case it is very convenient to assume there was a shortcut involved because you only know very little to nothing about the winner and how if not with the help of a shortcut should he/she be able to achieve something that you can't (at the moment)?
It is only when you win yourself that you realize how much work is needed in order to get there. It is consistent effort that will lead you to a win and not a shortcut because the journey is the reward or in other words, the consistent effort is what enables you to do something that other only dream of.
The win itself is just the moment where the others realize what you are capable of!
