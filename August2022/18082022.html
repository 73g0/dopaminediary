dopamine diary : 

golf is life

18th of August, 2022


Bobby Jones, a famous american golf player, once said: "Golf is the closest game to the game we call life. You get bad breaks from good shots; you get good breaks from bad shots - but you have to play the ball where it lies."

The more you practice, the better your misses will be and for me that is what life is about.
Living life to the fullest while always being prepared to miss. If I am, my misses are not going to mess up the whole thing and that's what I want!
