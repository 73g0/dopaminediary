dopamine diary : 

purpose is what it's all about

10th of August, 2022


Watch the YouTube-video "Navy Seal Commander explains why wake up at 4am" from Casey Neistat.
At one point in the video Casey states that purpose is what it's all about and he is right!

In the past I have tried very often to wake up very early in the morning consistently and I have also failed very often. I have read books, watched videos or listened to podcasts about how to do it and have come to the conclusion that everything comes down to one realization: You need a purpose!
You need a purpose that is strong enough to get you out of bed without hesitation and without a thought about how tired you are.
This purpose should be a "SMART" goal (specific, measurable, attainable, realistic, fixed time frame). For me goals like "be able to run 10km in a single run in one month", which would tick all the SMART boxes is not urgent enough. I need goals that I can achieve directly after the effort, e.g. "run 5km before 07:00 in order to be awake and alert for studying". I recommend trying this approach. It feels like a superpower!
